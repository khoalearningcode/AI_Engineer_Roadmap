Interview Checklist

Questions to answer with projects, not theory alone

RAG System Design

  • How do you design a RAG chatbot for 10,000 internal documents?
  • How do you evaluate retrieval quality?
  • How do you reduce hallucination?
  • When do you use reranking?
  • How do you handle document updates?

LLM Inference

  • What is KV cache?
  • What is the difference between prefill and decode?
  • What is continuous batching?
  • How do you measure LLM serving performance?
  • How does quantization affect quality and memory?

AI Platform

  • How do you design an API gateway for LLM serving?
  • How do you log prompts, retrieval results, and model responses?
  • How do you handle latency and timeout?
  • How do you deploy with Docker?
  • How do you monitor GPU utilization?

NVIDIA/GPU Basics

  • Why does a model run out of GPU memory?
  • What is mixed precision?
  • What is TensorRT used for?
  • What is Triton Inference Server?
  • When would you use ONNX?

Multimodal AI

  • How does CLIP image-text retrieval work?
  • How do you evaluate retrieval models?
  • What is the difference between image-text retrieval and VQA?
  • How can OCR and LLM be combined?
  • How do you deploy a vision model efficiently?
